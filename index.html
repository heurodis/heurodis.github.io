Saving Dover Forest

A resource site for the conservation of Dover Forest in Ulu Pandan, Singapore

Dover Forest is a 33-hectare 50-year-old secondary forest situated between three roads and a canal - the major roads Commonwealth Avenue West and Clementi Road, the minor road Ghim Moh Link, and the concretised canal section of Sungei Ulu Pandan. The forest is divided into western and eastern patches by a mowed grass lawn and a covered walkway linking Dover MRT station and Sungei Ulu Pandan.

Dover Forest is zoned for residential use. Parts of its eastern patch next to Ghim Moh Link estate may soon be cleared for a HDB BTO project. This website aims to be a comprehensive resource centre for the conservation of the forest.
